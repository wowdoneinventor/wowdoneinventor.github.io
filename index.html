Ants-facts

The first ants were 130 million years ago. These babies are as old as dinosaurs!
Ants inhabit the entire surface of the Earth, with the exception of Antarctica and a few islands.
Ants have two stomachs. Insects use one stomach to digest food. And they need the second in order to accumulate food, which can be shared with another member of the colony!
